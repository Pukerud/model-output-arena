🌻 Lawn Defense 🧟

A tiny Plants vs Zombies. Collect sun, grow your defenses, and survive all 10 waves of the undead.

Built in a single HTML file · no libraries

How to Play

☀️ Sun drops from the sky and from Sunflowers. Click it to collect (25 each).
🌱 Buy a plant by clicking a seed packet, then click an empty lawn tile to plant.
🟢 Peashooter & ❄️ Snow Pea shoot the lane in front of them.
🌻 Sunflower makes sun. 🥜 Wall-nut soaks damage. 🍒 Cherry Bomb clears a 3×3 area.
🧟 Zombies walk left. If one reaches your house, the lawnmower in that row saves you — once.
🏁 Survive 10 escalating waves (every 3rd is a Huge Wave) to win.
⌨️ Shortcuts: 1–5 pick seed · Esc cancel · P pause · M mute
