Plants vs Zombies

Defend the lawn through 10 waves. Collect sun, plant defenses, survive.

Tip: click a seed card, then click a lawn tile to plant.

How to Play

• Sun is your currency. It falls from the sky and is made by Sunflowers. Click it to collect.
• Click a seed card (top bar), then click an empty lawn tile to plant.
• Peashooter shoots peas down its lane. Wall-nut soaks damage. Cherry Bomb explodes a 3×3 area.
• Zombies march left. If they reach the house, a lawnmower clears that lane — once.
• Survive all 10 waves (every 3rd is a Huge Wave). Clear them all to win.
• Right-click or Esc cancels the selected seed.
