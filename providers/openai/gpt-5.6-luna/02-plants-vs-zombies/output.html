Three steps to dawn

This tiny game follows a simple plan:

  • Click a seed card, then place it on an empty tile.
  • Collect the falling sunlight by clicking it on the lawn.
  • Balance economy and defense before the zombies reach the left edge.

Each wave gets faster. Sunflowers are your economy, Peashooters attack, Repeaters attack twice, and Wall-nuts buy precious time.
