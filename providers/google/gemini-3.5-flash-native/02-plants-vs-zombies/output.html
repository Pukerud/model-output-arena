How to Play

☀️ Collect Sun: Click on falling suns or suns produced by Sunflowers to build up energy.

🌱 Plant Defenders: Select a card from the top menu, then click any grid cell on the lawn to place it.

🧟 Fight Waves: Prevent zombies from reaching the left side of the screen. If a zombie breaks through, the lawnmower will activate as a last defense!

📋 Seed Packets:

  • 🌻 Sunflower (50 Sun): Generates suns periodically.
  • 🌱 Peashooter (100 Sun): Shoots peas straight ahead.
  • 🛡️ Wall-nut (50 Sun): Extremely tough, blocks zombies.
  • 🌿 Repeater (200 Sun): Fires double peas.
  • 🍒 Cherry Bomb (150 Sun): Explodes instantly, clearing a 3x3 grid.
