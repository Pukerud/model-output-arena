🌻 GARDEN DEFENDERS 🧟

a mini Plants vs. Zombies — defend the house through 8 waves

1–5 select seeds · click lawn to plant · Esc/P pause · M mute

How to play

Zombies shamble in from the right. Don't let them reach your house on the left!

  • ☀️ Collect sun — click suns that fall from the sky or pop out of Sunflowers.
  • 🌱 Plant — click a seed packet (or press 1–5), then click a lawn tile.
  • 🌻 Sunflower (50) makes sun  ·  🟢 Peashooter (100) shoots
  • ❄️ Snow Pea (175) slows  ·  🥜 Wall-nut (50) blocks  ·  🍒 Cherry Bomb (150) boom!
  • 🪓 Shovel — select it, then click a plant to dig it up.
  • 🚜 Lawn mowers — one per lane, a last-resort zombie wipe. Lose one lane's mower and the next breach ends the game.
  • 🌊 Survive all 8 waves (the last one is huge) to win. Score = kills + waves + sun + surviving mowers.
