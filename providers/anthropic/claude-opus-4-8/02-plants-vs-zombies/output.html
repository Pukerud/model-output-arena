Garden Defenders

A mini Plants vs Zombies — survive 10 waves of the undead.
Plant Sunflowers for sun, Peashooters to attack, and Wall-nuts to block. Don't let a zombie reach your house!

How to play

☀️ Sun is your currency. Sunflowers make it; some also falls from the sky.

🌻 Sunflower (50) — generates sun over time. Plant these first.

🌱 Peashooter (100) — fires peas at zombies in its lane.

🧊 Snow Pea (175) — fires frost peas that slow zombies.

🥥 Wall-nut (50) — a tough blocker that soaks up damage.

Select a plant from the top bar, then click a tile to plant it. Click the shovel to remove a plant. Survive all 10 waves to win.
